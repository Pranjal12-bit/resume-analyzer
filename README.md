## ✨ **Features**

### 🤖 **AI-Powered Analysis**
- **Smart Resume Parsing** - Extract text from PDF and DOCX files
- **ATS Score Calculation** - Get a score out of 10 for ATS compatibility
- **Keyword Detection** - Identify missing keywords for your target role
- **Formatting Feedback** - Catch grammar and formatting issues

### 👤 **User Management**
- **Secure Authentication** - JWT-based login/registration
- **Personal Dashboard** - Track all your resume analyses
- **History Tracking** - View past uploads and scores

### 📊 **Detailed Reports**
- **Tabbed Analysis** - Overview, Suggestions, Keywords, Formatting
- **Visual Score** - Color-coded ATS meter (🔴 Low, 🟡 Medium, 🟢 High)
- **PDF Export** - Download detailed feedback reports

### 🎨 **Beautiful UI**
- **Responsive Design** - Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Modern Interface** - Clean, professional design
- **Loading States** - Smooth animations and skeletons
- **Error Handling** - User-friendly error messages
